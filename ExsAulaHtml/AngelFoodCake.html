Angel's Food Cake

Ingredients|| Preparation

Ingredients

Serves up to 12 people

  • 12 Large Egg Whites (room temperature)
  • 1 and 1/2 teaspoons cream of tartar
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 cup powdered sugar

Preparation
  1. Pre-heat Oven to 350ºF (aprox. 175ºC)
  2. Separate the yolks from the egg whites
  3. Start whiping the egg Whites (low speed until foamy bubbles have formed)
  4. Add the Cream of Tartar and salt to the egg whites increase whipping speed, and continue until the egg whites form soft peaks
  5. Add the sugar Slowly and the vanilla extract
  6. Increase Whiping speed, whip until stiff peaks form
  7. Turn off the Mixer
  8. Sift in the flour and powdered sugar
  9. Using a spatula and Very gentely fold the the sugar and flour into the egg white mix
  10. Once the ingredients are fully incorporated is time to scoop the batter into a cake tin (Ungreased!)
  11. Bake for 40 - to 45 minutes
  12. Since the tin was ungreased you'll have to use a thin knife to loosen the cake from the tin

This is a very light and fluffy cake the folding process can be a bit tricky but otherwise its a very simple recipe. Use the eggyolks to make some Doce de Ovos and use as a sauce for the cake
